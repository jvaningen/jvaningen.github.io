Resizing Text in Your Browser


So what happens if you're on a website that does not have a text resizer? By managing the settings of your browser, you can ensure that your preferences in terms of text size are displayed no matter what website you visit.


Each browser is different, but here are some tips on text resizing in some of the most popular browsers:


Internet Explorer


  1. 1) Go to the "View" menu
  2. 2) Scroll down to "Text Size", where you can adjust font size from very small to very large.

Firefox


  1. 1) Go to the "View" menu
  2. 2) Scroll down to "Text Size", where you can adjust font size from very small to very large.

Safari


  1. 1) Go to the "View" menu
  2. 2) Scroll down to "Make Text Bigger" or "Make Text Smaller".

Chrome


  1. 1) Click on settings icon in top-right corner.
  2. 2) From the menu bar, click on settings.
  3. 3) Click on advanced settings.
  4. 4) Scroll down to "Web Content", where you can adjust font size from very small to very large.
