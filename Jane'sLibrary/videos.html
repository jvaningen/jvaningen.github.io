Lulu


When my partner was recovering from surgery, she was missing our beloved miniature poodle, Lulu. In an effort to lift her spirits, I emailed her videos every day, and this is my favorite one.


I love how she shakes herself off and goes on to the next thing. It's a good philosophy to have in life.


I can create and edit videos for non-profits, too, and add them to websites, social media and email to increase visibility and awareness about a non-profit's mission.
